Interesting Facts

Tower in Paris, France

Paris is the capital of France and is known for its beautiful architecture, art, fashion, and cusine. It is also famous for its world-renowned museums and gallaries, such as the Louvre and the Eiffel Tower. According to Wikipedia, tourism continued to recover in the Paris region in 2022, increasing to 44 million visitors, an increase of 95 percent over 2021, but still 13 percent lower than in 2019.
Hawaii, Honolulu, Waikiki

According to wikipedia, the largest city and airport in the Hawaiian Islands, Honolulu acts as a natural gateway to the islands' large tourism industry, which brings millions of visitors and contributes $10 billion annually to the local economy. Honolulu's favorable tropical climate, rich natural scenery, and extensive beaches make it a popular global destination for tourists.
Building in Vancouver, Canada

Some of the best places to visit in Vancouver are Grouse Mountain, Rogers Arena, Stanley Park, etc. Vancouver is also known for its beautiful beaches, including the famous Balmoral Beaach. According to wikipedia, each year over a million people pass through Vancouver on cruise ship vacations, often bound for Alaska. Vancouver is also one of the wettest Canadian cities.
